Egg Rice

Description

This simple two ingredient egg rice is my favourite food in the whole world.

Ingredients

  • Egg(s)
  • Cooked Rice

Steps

  1. Take a pan and make scrambled eggs with salt and chilli powder
  2. Put cooked rice in it
  3. Add salt again
  4. Mix everthing without breaking the rice
  5. You can also add anything extra like soy sauce, pepper, vegetables or anthing you want
  6. Enjoy
